A Quarral with my wife:
Few days ago, in a busy day, my wife and I worked together to prepare for the lunch.
I wash the vegies, shrimps, she perpared cold dishes with spicy integrants. 
Many times, after she eat spicy food, she got stomach ache and expressed regretful for eating the spicy.
But after she recovered, she eat spicy again.  
 
I gentlly said that spicy food is not good for her. Spicy is suject to make people hot temper.
We should not eat food for good taste but for netrition. 
 
But she got angry: "Are you hungry? if not, go back to your room.
I felt very hurt. I cared for her, but she angry at me without any reason.
 
I responsed: "I can cooperate with you in cooking, and any routine work. But I cannot cooperate with you fire anger.
If what I said is wrong, you can correct me. I do not want fire. I want live."
 
I went back my room, my heart is blooding. I complained to Jesus, why did you give me a wife like this?
 
Jesus did not say anyword to me at that moment, but He approached to me with love and peace without saying anything to me.
I suddenly felt guilty.
 
What Jesus tolerated in the world is much much hard than I did. As comparion, yours is nothing. Why should I complain?
On the other hand, I am deserved to be treated like that or even worse. I am selfish, self-pride, self-centered person.
 
Each time after worship or fellowship, my heart is full of joy. But with my wife, I often got anger with my wife.
As a comparison, I love church and fellowship more than my wife. I love Jesus word more than my wife's words, and any words in the world.
 
The pain in heart made me to love Jesus more.
 
And the more I love Jesus, the less my pain was,  I became to love my wife more.
I clearly know that this love is different from the my old worldly love, but the love of Jesus. 
 
If she treats me well, if our life is as sweet as in the romantic movie, will I followed Jesus to go to missionary?
I doubt it. From this point I thank my wife, I thank Jesus. 
 
Jesus is great, so great and the greatest.  
 
 
Act 4:12.
"Salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to men by which we must be saved." (Act4:12 NIV) 
"除他以外、别无拯救．因为在天下人间、没有赐下别的名、我们可以靠着得救。" (Act4:12 CUVS)
 
A few days ago, my wife and I had chance to eat with a Taiwanese in a dinner. His name is J. He graduated from GS in the chemical dept and now works in a company. He said he does not believe in any religion but he respects all kinds of religions.
He told me that there are two kinds of Taiwanese in Taiwan: One is from the mainland before 1949. another is the natiave.  He was a native Taiwanese. His family came to Taiwan about four hundred years ago. 
He knows Taiwan culture a lot.  He said, he doesn't like GMT party from mainland China. He supports independence of Taiwan. 
 
Following his topic and his view point, I said:
"I also support independence of Taiwan.
But I don't like Taiwan Presidential Inauguration Ceremony. Because it is full of the idolship. They bowed down to the picture of Sun who died a hundred years ago. 
We can respect him, but we should not bow down to his picture. God resents the idolship. That kind of ceremony will not bring the blessing but lead to tragedy."
 
He said:"I don't like the ceremony too. I epseically don't like the Taiwanese national flag. It is indeed a symbol for GMT party. However, These are part of the institution inherited from GMT party. No once can change it."
 
Howeveer, he also said that "Taiwan is a freedom contry. There are many different religions like Daoism, Buddism, Islam, Catholic and Christianity etc in Taiwan. People can freely choose whatever he wants as long as he feels good. "
 
 
I said:"I have experienced the communism and buddhism, Daoism. My experience told me that Christianity is the only correct one." 
 
He said:"That is your experience. You cannot represent all."
 
I said: "There exist only one truth among all religions." I quoted act 4:12. 
"On one hand, a good country is not about its size or population, or its richness, it is about the happiness of the people, the peace of the people. Only the people who believe in God can be blessed. Taiwanese has only two percent of Christians, I hope Taiwanese can make use of the freedom to pursue Jesus Christ, so that Taiwanese can be saved. "
 
"On the other hand, the salvation is about the personal eternal life. What we believe is the most important thing for our life." 
 
I think my conversation can lead him to think more about Christianity. At leaset I released an important message of the verse act 4:12.
 
Audience: youth Taiwanese.
My life is full of suffering. 
I used to think I am the most miserable people in the world. But after I was reborn, I felt that I am the happiest person in the world.
