My Spiritual Journey So Far
William Ding. 2020-12-2
God love me, but I didn't know; God had a plan for me, but I almost missed it.
  1. 1964
    (1) According to my mother's account, I was born in Beijing Xuanwu Hospital in 1964. The hospital is close to The Xuanwu Gate Catholic Church that was built by Matteo Ricci in 1605. (http://oa.upm.es/33835/1/SHAN_HUANG.pdf).  I visited both sites in Dec 2019.
    (2) At the age of three, due to the movement of the cultural revolution, I was sent to my grandmom's family in a small town at Yiwu of Zhejiang Province.
     
  2. 1972
    I was picked up by my parents and began the painful education journey with my parents in Beijing: kindergarten, elementary, middle and high school.
     
  3. 1982
    (1)Left parents again and began to study mechanical engineering in the south of China -- during the period, I learned Chinese Zhuangzi. 
    (2) in 1986, I continued to pursue the master's degree major in vibration control.
     
  4.  1989
    (1) During my defense period for my master's degree, the Tiananmen massacre occurred on June 4th,1989.  I faced a tough decision to make between the master's degree diploma and the freedom, justice or truth, between the surviving and death. 
    (2) It is a turning point of my original materialism, marxism and communism faith. The three love became three hatred in my life. My goal of life was to leave China forever. Trump is my reminder of such trauma.
     
  5. 1992
    (1) I mysteriously left Beijing Capital Iron and Steel Company and started to work for Univeral Instrument Co USA at Beijing office. Visited and was trained in Binghamtom NY in Feb 1992. It was my first trip to go abroad and in USA. If I could come to USA once, I could come twice. USA is my personal dream. 
    (2) met my wife in 1992 after I came back from Binghamton.
    (3) Couple of days after my son my son was born in Feb 6th, 1993, I went to Binghamton again. 
  6. 1995
    (1) Followed wife's student visa and went to Atlanta Ga. USA
    (2) 2000, graduated from Southen Tech (MSCS), then worked for FATS rather than Seimens.
    (3) In the mid of 2000, after being laid off from FATS, I worked for Galaxy Engineering in Alpharetta.
    (4) sent my son to Chinese Sunday School in Perimeter church, began to rethink  the truth of the Christian faith as well as the Chinese culture.
  7. 2003
    (1) got citizenship.
    (2) laid off 
    (3) family war broke out.
    (4) found a job in Ligos Software in Savannah GA.
    (5) fought to God. paralyzed for three months.
    (6) turned to Jesus and baptized in Dec 2003 in ACCC.
    (7) worked in Motorolla of Atlant(2005-2006), Selectron (2007-2008),  Emerson of Huntsville (2011-2016), 
     
  8. 2016
    (1) quitted Emerson, left Huntsville to go to CIU seminary school.
    (2) visited Beijing in Dec 2019.
    (3) Delima on the way of missionary.
