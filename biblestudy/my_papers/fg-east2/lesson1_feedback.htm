第一課（真理與文化）：如果”懂得知識”的文化取代了”見証福音”的真理，在教會中會産生什麽問題？ *
(1) Culture, whether it is the Chinese culture, American culture or Jewish culture, whether it is Christianity culture or non-Chrisitanity culture, always belongs to the world. Any culture will be melt down and pass away. However, the truth of the Gospel existed before the world and any culture, and it is the source of our life, and will exists forever and ever. Jesus said that: "Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will never pass away." (Mat24:35 NIV) "天地要废去、我的话却不能废去。" (Mat24:35 CUVS) If we replace the church with knowledge and culture, then out church cannot be called the church any more. It can only be called an association,  a committee or a non-profit organization.  
(2) Culture and the truth cannot be divided in our life. At one hand, we are born and grew up in a culture. our physical life depend on the culture to maintain. On the other hand, Christians are reborn in the Holy Spirit. We no longer belong to this world. We are the children of the God, citizens of the kingdom of Heaven. Whereas, we cannot totally remove or kill any culture. Christian living on the earth is to master the culture to use it to preach the Gospel,  truth of the Holy Spirit, to glorify God. Culture has powerful influence in the world. But Christian must defeat the chains of any culture and let the culture to serve for God. Behind the culture it is the evil, the Serpent and the Dragon. Along with the truth is the Holy Spirit, Jesus Christ who is the Son of God.  The battle between the culture and the truth belongs to God and Jesus had already won from the death on cross. If Christians ignored the Gospel and replace it with college degrees and all kinds of titles of the world, then we are not serving the Lord, especially for Chinese Christians,  they are subject to love and glorify the Chinese culture and become a shy Chinese person.
 
第二課（榮耀的教會）：您對楊牧師所強調”福音是教會最重要的使命”認同嗎？ *
請說出上題您如此選擇的理由是？ *
Completely Agree:
Completely Agree: (1) The Gospel is the core of Christianity.
(2) The Gospel is the only truth in the world.
(3) The Gospel is the only way to redeem the sinners to lead Christians to break the prison of the culture to enter into the Kingdom of Heaven.
There is no other ways that can save us. We are reborn through the Gospel, by the Gospel and for the Gospel.   
 
您個人覺得楊牧師這兩次課的信息包括問題解答，所傳遞的觀念對ACCC見證平衡的福音有什麼幫助？ *
In the Gospel, there is no concept of the balance (平衡) between the Gospel and the culture/world. In ACCC or Chinese missionary fields, we either preach and glorify the truth of the Gospel to expose the evilness of Chinese culture in public, or we make use of the Gospel to develop and unwittingly glorify the Chinese culture. There is no middle balanced ground here between the two.  Chinese Christians should not keep any Chinese face but fully commit themselves to Christ. "耶稣说、手扶着犁向后看的、不配进　神的国。" (Luk9:62 CUVS) One takeaway from Pastor Yang is that we should not fear the powerful Chinese culture, but "I press on toward the goal to win the prize for which God has called me heavenward in Christ Jesus." (Phl3:14 NIV) "向着标竿直跑、要得　神在基督耶稣里从上面召我来得的奖赏。" (Phl3:14 CUVS) He specifically reiterated that there is only one goal rather two or more balanced goals. ACCC should be more bravely to raise the Gospel and use the truth as the sword to expose the injustice, liars and wick influences from Chinese culture, and liberate His people free from the ruling or influencing from the ancient traditional culture,  just like what Moses did to lead his people to get rid of Egyptian Pharisees to enter into the Canaan land that God promised.
