Only two kinds of loves as noun in CUV.

  1. 慈爱:197x, great kindness, steadfast love, unfailing kindness,
  2. 仁爱:8x, love; agape

What is 仁爱 in Chinese?

  1. 仁 is one of the most foundmental concept and word in Chinese culture system.
  2. not found in Oracles; created in 300 BC.
  3. it is discussed and disputed most in China history.
  4. Confusius and Daoism use this word.
  5. It is one of the most confusing word in Chinese.

The key difference between 仁爱 and 神爱

  1. both claim to love people.
  2. Jesus claim to love your neighbor not people.
  3. Jesus' love came from God, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Whereas, Chinese love(仁爱) came from blood, flesh and man's will.
  4. while using the Chinese "仁爱", the chinese love also entered into human's heart.
  5. Some may make use of "仁爱" in CUV to praise and glorify and develope Chinese culture rather than the Lord.
  6. Recommended not to use "仁爱" but replace it with "神爱" in CUV.
