Introduction

The object-oriented analysis (OOA) was originally a trival method appeared as terms of 'object' and 'oriented' in artificial intelligence engineering during late 1950's. However it suddenly became world-wilde popular terminology during 1990' due to that it was successfully applied into software enginneering to break the traditional procedurals framework in mind and to replace the Lines-Of-Codes (LOC) quantities accumulation process with human's unlimited abstractive concepulation process.  Today OOA is one of most important foundimental theories and methods not only in computer science, but also in many other areas, like phylosophy, liguishtic, biology etc. It could be said that today's software industries would probably still stayed back at a level of DOS command line without OOA.

Object and Class

In English, 'object' means a material thing that can be seen and touched.  (Synonyms are: aim - purpose - article - objective - goal.) In OOA there is no extra special definition for 'object' since the meaning descripted in English dictionary is clear enough.

Objects can be many different types. Some of the objects are uniqueness, like the sun, the moon etc. These object are called singleton in OOA. Some of objects have capability of self-reproduction, like animal, or plants. Some of objects are not countable and have no fixed forms like air, water. Some of object could not be seen and touched directly but could be detected with equipments, like gravity field, photons etc.

No matter what types of objects, they all have common features called 'existance', or 'being'. The study of the nature of being, existence, or reality, as well as the basic categories of being and their relations.will have to involve in the philosophical subject called ontology(本体论). More fundamental study of being and world could fall into more broaden areas called metaphysic where the discussion of common topics could be endlessly. Because all objects in OOA are conreate/specific instances of abstract/general ideas called 'class'.  In a more formal definition, a class in OOA is a blueprint or prototype/plan/ template from which the individual objects are created.

The relationship between object and class in OOA are a kind of relationship between the concreate and the abstrace, the specific and the general. Inheritance

The inheritance is an important concept in OOA regarding about the relationship among classes, wherein a child class shares the attributes or behaviour of its parents or ancestors. Through inheritance relationship all classes can be virtually linked together to form a family with hierarchy tree. There are two types of inheritance: deriving and compositing. However their primary common purpose of inheritance is to get the reusability of attributes or behaviours among different classes.
 
Seldomly is discussed about the relationship among objects in OOA. Since all objects can be freely cloned or self-reproduced if it is not a singleton, to discuss the relationship among objects are evitable. Druing the objects population process, the state of parents object can natually be passed into children objects. In order to differentiate the concept of inheritance, we call this phynomena as 'heredity'.
 
Although both inheritance and heredity are talking about something passing on between parents and children, with OOA introduced in discussion, they are essentially different. Inheritance refer to the attributes or behviours in parents classes that are permitted to reuse by children classes as if the children owned those attributes and behaviours. Heredity refer to the value of attributes or the state of behaviours when duplicated from parents to children. Inheritance passes along the access permission disscussed in encapsulation. Heredity passes on the values of attributes. Inheritance is taling about the copy right. Heredity is talking about the copy value. Inheritance belongs to metaphysics world. Heredity belongs to physics world. And it is more important that inheritance is a design time process, whereas heredity is run-time process.  They are totally talking about two different concepts.

Any modern languages, its writing system contains three essentially different rudimental elements. They are words, pronunciations and definitions.

Pronunciation denotes the aural sound or voice of a word when people speak and can be hear though human ears and identified by human brain. A pronunciation has to have a definition. A pronunciation that has no definitions is the noise.

 

Definition denotes the concept, ideas, or meaning. It is the unit of human cognation. A definition is the perception of the existence in the world. All languages share some basic common definitions with different words and different pronunciations. If they do not share any common definitions, the translations among languages is impossible.

 

Word and pronunciation are two different types of mediums in definition message transmission. During the human language communication process, a word itself is a visual carrier of the definitions, whereas a pronunciation is an aural carrier of the definitions. The definition information can be transmitted and received either through the light of word or the acoustic of pronunciation. In spite that both word and pronunciation can not exist without their definitions, a word and a pronunciation can be separated and exist independently on each other.

 

Although word, pronunciation and definition are the most fundamental elements in linguistics, they do not have internal mechanism to unify three elements together into one object. This lead to two types of languages in the world. They are alphabetic and ideographic languages.

 

The alphabetic languages is a written human language in which thousands or unlimited number of all different words are composed of several dozens of standardized set of alphabetic letters � basic simple written symbols. In alphabetic language, there is no any physical or conceptual natural relationship between the words and definitions. The corresponding relationship between words and definition is thoroughly arbitrarily defined or determined by human conventions � dictionary. Hence, people has to remember large mount of definitions of words in brain. The biggest advantage of alphabetic language is that alphabetic languages can easily setup a relationship between the words and its pronunciations through the simple alphabet-sound table. So people can easily reproduce correct pronunciation through the words with simple rules without looking up the dictionary. The alphabetic language in which symbols or letters reflect the pronunciation of the words is also called phonetic language. Examples are Latin, English, Greek, Russian, Thai, Arabic and Hebrew. Alphabetic words are also called phonetic words.
