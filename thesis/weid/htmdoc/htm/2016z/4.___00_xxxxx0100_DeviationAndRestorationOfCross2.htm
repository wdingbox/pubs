Today whenever people saw the CROSS, the first mind that Chinese people will come up is just a number ten without other meaning. But in TBI times, the CROSS has two meanings.

  2. the first of heaven-sterm (see table )

One word denotes two completely different meaning which is nearly conflicting to each other.  This is a very special case that exist in TBI. Why not to use two different symbols to represent two different words with different meaning to simply the reading and writing?  There are tons of free symbols to pick up or define at that times.

The results of battle is out of what we expected. The number ten involved in and take away the symbol of the CROSS. The original symbol for both number 7 and the first of heaven-stem was changed to the state as we use today.
