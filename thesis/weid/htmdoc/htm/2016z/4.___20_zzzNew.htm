Human Being In TBI

All word used to descript the human being is listed in table. From the table we can find that:

  1. The front view of human being, which we think that is most similar to human outlok, is not used to represent human. Instead, they represent king, heaven, master etc.
  2. The side view of human being with worship style on the kneel is used to denote human being.
  3. The side view of lower part and front view of upper part of femal are combined together in one picture and they are used to represent femal person. The one with nipples are used to represent mother. The one without nipple should represent the virgin.
  4. Father, son and human male are denoted by logograms with cross.
  5. The side view of human being without worship style, but looks like a snake, is used to represent corpse or dead person.

We can further find that:

  1. Father and mother are completely different in the way to express in TBI. One is pictogram, another is logogram. Father and mother does not appear in pair.
  2. Human male and femal are denoted with complete diffferent method. The male is picotgram, the female is logogram.
  3. There exist word of son, but not word to denote daughter.
  4. There exist elder brother, but not word for elder sister.

Using completely different attitudes and methods to decripe the same mature human being with different gender are called gender asymmetrics phenomimoea (GAP).   However, GAP is different from femal discrimination in china history.
