Compared with thousands of all all different characters used between OBI times and modern times of today, only about eight characters were remained unchanged in writing format.  The first three characters in Nu documents were the only three characters survived without any changes both in writing and in meanings in three thousands years old history.  The Chinese Cross Character (CCC) was the only character we known that its writing style was kept, but the meaning was totally changed.
