TBI Consolidation

The process to consolidate the similar variant characters is called the consolidation process. There are two steps in consolidation process:

  1. Find out all words that have similar writing styles, and put them together into a group.
  2. Choose the most frequently used word in the group as the character to represent that group.

Through consolidation process, we can get about distinct 3809~3948 individual characters from 46,635 variant styles of characters[SongZhenHao]. But this paper will mainly use about 5,450 from about 6,000 characters from Chant.org and Chineseetymology.org.

 

 

 

 

Scripture Grouping:During the analysis process, we found that many words its differences in writing are so subtle that we can not found any differences in meaning. Hence we put these very similar words together in one group and choose a commonely used one for their representitive.

 

  1. Meaning Grouping: Different from grouping in writing, we also found some cases that the writing are completely diferent, but they have been intereted as same meaning for scholars. We alos put these words in one group and choose the common one as their representitive.

 

There are two steps in grouping process:

  1. Find out all words that have same meaning, and put them together as a group.
  2. Find out the most frequently used word in the group, and  choose that one as  the leader of the group or group representitive.
