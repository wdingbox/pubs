Introduction

From OOA viewpoints, there is no difference between logograms and pictograms -- all characters are the children of root 'one'. Depending on the visual thinking, a character can be both pictogram and logogram. Character 'one' for example, when we think of it in abstraction in math, philosophy, religions etc, it belongs to logogram. If we think of it as a horizontal sea line, then it belong to pictogram.

However, from the characters searching viewpoints, the logograms has simple and clear tree structures and hence is better than the pictogram in OOA system. For searching the tangible objects for pictograms, the shoping catagories is still the best way.
