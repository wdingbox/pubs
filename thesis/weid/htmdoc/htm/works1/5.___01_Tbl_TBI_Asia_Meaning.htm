About Asia

In English, Asia is  from Latin Asia (1300 AD), from Greek Asia, speculated to be from Akkadian asu "to go out, to rise," in reference to the sun, thus "the land of the sunrise."

In western civilization, they all called east continents as 'Asia'. Asia means 'east'. But people in the so called east continents call himself as . Many scholars believe that it is the original word of today's simplied chinese word 亚(ya3), traditional chinese word 亞,  it is equvalent to 'Asia' in English.
