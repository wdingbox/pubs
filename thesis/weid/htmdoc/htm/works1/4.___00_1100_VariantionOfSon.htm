Nearly fifty different words in TBI are used to denote the son. As compared with the variation of CROSS, the SON is much more comlicated. However it provide more info related to gospel.

For the same format of word , there are two different denotions in TBI times:

  1. The biological son, child, baby.
  2. The sixth of the twelve Terrestrial Branches, represent time from 9 to 11 a.m.  The word for this meaning have been changed to be as  巳 in writing today.

 

For the same meaning regarding about biological son or child, this word has many different format or writing scriptures.

  1. Triangle-Cross: this word is very similar in writing with nailed-cross SON. In TBI times it denoted the eighth of the ten celestial stems. The word of this meaning have been converted into 辛 today.
  2. Baby Son: This writing is more similar in picture than other word representing SON. This type of word became popular in bronze times.
  3. Arising Son: This word is more pictorial than logo. It descripting a kind of state of flying or arising. The arrows in word    clearly told us that this flying is toward to upside heavenly. This word is still in use today. it means to die young.
  4. Chrown of Thrown: it is more pictorical than logo. It is very similar to the chrown of thrown.
  5. Son in Manger/Trough: This is a word in picture with side view of manger or trough which can be still seen in today's countryside or livestock farm. This meaning of this word is kept until today. However the meaning at TBI times, it also represent the first in twelve earthly branches.

 

Both in the forms of word and the meaning of the word we use today, we can find following key words closely related to word of SON:

  1. cross
  2. heaven
  3. earth
  4. baby
  5. arising
  6. to die at young age
  7. chrown of throne

These key words or phrases are also the key word used in the story in gospel. There are no second book in the world can match as much closely as gospel.

We can clonclude that when talking about the son in TBI times, it is not regarding about the generic biological child of common human being. It was specially talking about a special son. In another words, the SON is not originated to descritping the every child of human being. If we study the friends of son, like preganant, it can further aprove this.

If we look into it we can find that almost all word used in stem and branch are related to the gospel. Three of them are CROSS related; two of them are nails; four of them are ancient weapon; one of them is the word of GOD, snake, wood, wine container.
