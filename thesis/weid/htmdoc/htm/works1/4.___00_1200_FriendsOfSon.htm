Friends Of Son In TBI

 

There are about 100 words made of nail-cross-son and about 20 words made from triangle-cross-cross. (see table)  Other types of son are used as standalone. Among the words made from son, some of them can be interchangeable.

The friends of son provide more evidences to prove that the son is not a common son people think in daily life.

 

1) The son of man

In TBI, there is a gender asymmetric phenomena.

  1. There are different types of words for son, but no words to denotes daughter. There is no such word that a female is in the body of man or woman to represent girl.
  2. The word 'pregnant' comes from the son in the body of a generic man, not woman nor mother. In TBI, what the word 'pregnant' want to denote is 'son of man'.
  3. Another word express 'pregnant'

The word 'pregnant'  is still in use till today.
