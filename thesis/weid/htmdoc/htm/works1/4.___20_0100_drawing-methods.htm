Pictogram's Drawing Techniqe

As compred with logogram, pictogram is easier to reconize since they are directly drawn from the real existing visial objects. To kKnow the basic painting techniqus can further help us to better understand the meaning of pictograms. Following simple drawing techniques were found to be used in pictogram in TBI.

  1. Orthographic Projection Drawing.   
    a.  Front View,
    b.  Side View,
    c.  Top View,
    d.  Back View
    e:  Intersection View
  2. Perspective Drawing.
    a. One Point Linear Perspective View

Any object in a space can be observed in directions of front, back, left/right side, top and bottonm. These directions are relative. The front view could be considered as back view. When reading the TBI, readers have to use their imaginations to try each of views it possible used and figure it out the drawing method that is actually used in order to accurate identify the object.  

Some of TBI, different drawing methods are used on the same object (for example, turtle). This is how synonyms comes from in TBI.
