Discoveries Inside of TBI

 

We found that each character, its writing structure, meaning, and usage, has defined relationships to others. None of the TBI characters is a purely isolated word in the writing system even though they seem to have no direct connection in meaning. To collect all of the internal connections among several thousands of different characters is as complicated as editing of a dictionary. However, we found that there exists a simplicity inside of the whole TBI writing system. Different from an Oxford dictionary that is a book that has no main idea, the TBI writing system appears a hidden central point of ideology through which it can help us to look up all others.

We represent the core thoughts of TBI writing system by discussion of a couple of most critical keywords. Once we uncover the hidden ideas inside of TBI writing system, readers can decode all others with the same integrated method. We found that the TBI dictionary is not necessary needed for us.
