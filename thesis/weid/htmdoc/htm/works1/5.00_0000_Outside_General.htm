Discoveries Outside TBI

The study of OBI writing system can link to many different areas like chinese or world history, archeology, astronomy, geology, philosophy, bronzes and stones, etc. This chapter collected the most prominent discoveries that has solid evidences outside of OBI writing system. Although these researches are not directly contributed to decode the meaning of inside of TBI, it formly supports the central ideaology discovered through analyzing the relationship inside of OBI system.  The consistency of discoveries from both inside and outside of OBI writing system undermined the core value of OBI writing system.
