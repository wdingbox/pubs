Pictogram Classifications And Analysises

In order to faciliate our studies and easily to track, we need to well organized the large amount messy characters in TBI.  Based on the common modern knowledge in our daily life, we use following hierachy model to classify all root pictograms into a structured tree.

  1.  living things 
    1. animals
           1. animal 
                    1. animal as whole (ox, lamb, horse, birds, turtle etc)
                    2. part of animal (head, body etc)
           2. humam
                   1. man as whole (man, woman, son etc.)
                   2. part of body  (head, eyes, ear, teeth, mouth, nose, hand, foot etc)
    2. plants (trees, rices etc)
  2. none-living things
    1. natural (sun, moon, rain, etc)
    2. man-made (clothes, food, housing, transportations, culture etc)
