Human And Animals In TBI Analysises
 

 1. Differences between human and animals in TBI.

Almost all animals we commonly see today including wild animals, livestocks, insects or birds in sky, fish in water or amphibians etc were inscriptured in the TBI. It is noticed that most of animals, especially for livestocks, wild animals walking on ground including human beings are pictured using side view method. Although there still exist difficulties to distinguish between similar animals, like donkey vs horses, the snapshot of side pictures successfully reflects the basic properties or features of all different classes of animals. For example, the one with long nose is elephant, the one with long hairs on the body are ox, the one with long horns is deer, the one with big eye and long tail is a horse, the one with round strip on the strong body is tiger etc, (see 5.1.1.1.4 in tree)

It mut be pointed out here, in TBI, the human being is clearly distinguished from animal thru side view of bowing down or on the kneel in worship style. This discovery not only tell us the life style of people in TBI times, that they they lived with holy life, but also inform us a message that the foundmental differences between the human and any ohter animals in the world is that they worship GOD. (refer to 5.1.a.m.n). Hence, in TBI, the corpse or dead person is picutured as a person who back is unable to bow down, and it looks like a snake.  (see 5.1.a.m.n.2.1.3)

2. No Differences between human and animal in flesh.

 The parts of body, like eye, elbow, nose, mouth, teeth,tonge, ear, face, hairs, head, hand, foot and so on, are commonly used to depict the detailed action, intention, or to express an idea or feeling for human being, seldomly used for animals. However, if we look at all TBI words that were used for parts of body, they all did not looks like images coming from the part of human body.   Large mount evidence show that the parts of body can be equally used for both human and animals in TBI. This fact demonstrated that the TBI originators considered the parts of human body same as the the body of animals. There is not differences between human and animals in flesh.

 4. Images of Face In TBI.

Among all images of parts of body, the most important one should be the image of the person's face. Only the image of face can show the feeling, the thought, and world view points. Face image is also the symbol of spirits and believes. But none of the TBI words, show any face or parts of face of any person. They seemed shamed for themself.  We only found the face as pictograms on goat, ox and snake. The most frequently and clearly face picogram is the face of a goat or a lamb. Both goat and ox face image are in top 400 most frequently used words in TBI. The face for snake is arguable and its frequency is as low as 1.
