In TBI, the SON has a 'girl friend'. This compound word is used until today. Its meaning is 'good'.

This word is commonly used in chinese daily life both in oral and in written. Many chinese people expalin that the reason that this word denote 'good' is because woman and son are what we desired.  This kind of explaination is baseless. First it is unfair to women. Second, there are more things than women and son that people disired in the world. Third, women and son may bring troubles for man rather than goodness.  

 ShuoWen: (說文解字): 美也從女子徐锴曰子者男子之美稱會意. ShuoWen interpret the son as man. ShuoWen explain woman and man as 'beauty'.

If you notice that the key words that exist in this words are SON, WOMAN, CROSS, NAIL, then you can find out that they perfectly match the key words in the story about the birth of Jesus. The word GOOD means the good news, - the saver of human came into the world. 

In deed this word can perfect match many famous early Eourope painting which expressed such meaning.
