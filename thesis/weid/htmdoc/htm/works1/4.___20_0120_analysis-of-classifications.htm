The classification we used here in picotograms is designed based on the common modern knowledge in our daily life. It is also deeply affected by the materialism that believe that the survive or exitsting of animal is determined by the perfect fit betweeinits capbilties and its living enviroment.

The first foundmental material that people can survive is the food. The sun and air are so aboundant that human can enjoy it without doing anything.  The daily required food however is not garanttted 7x24. It is also not as easy as air to get it whenever they need them. The fresh food is aboundant in natural in autum but not in winter. Human have to have a plan and work for food no matter thru the agriculture or livestock to maintain the life. People can choose a place with aboundatnt water and comfortable climate to ease their work and live. But so far we can not find any evidence that early human being have any capabilities or strong desire to choose to developt hybernating technique to maintain their life and survive.

The clothes and housing are the second foundmental materials for the people who lived in Yinxu (Longitude: 112.1872, Latitude: 37.8997, similar to New York in latitude) where the tempeature could reach to as low as -5 Centidegree. A person in regular clothing immersed in water just above freezing will last only a few minutes. Being in cold temperature air with proper shelter and clothing, humans can survive indefinatley, even if the temperature is below zero degrees, but it would be a bitter and painful life.

Based on the knowledge of modern science and tech, it is natural for us to think that the food, clothes and housing should be the center activities and works for human in order to maintain to live generation by gneration. However, from the freqencies of word and complixities of synonums of words in OBI tree, it show that what the they most concerned were  about the worship and war, we call it WW phenomenia. The WW phenomena told us that the threaten of life is not about the shortage of food, lack of clothes or housing, or earthquake, drownage, the wild animals, or the airbone illness, etc. The threaten of life came from human himself. The weapon are not primily used for hunting, but used for defencing and offencing. The vessals are not primirily used for daily food, but used for worship GOD.

 中国古代的青铜文化十分发达，并以制作精良，气魄雄伟、技术高超而著称于世。 奴隶主把青铜器作为宴享和放在宗庙里祭祀祖先的礼器。 青铜器不是一般人可以拥有的，它作为一种权利和地位的象征、一种记事耀功的礼器而流传于世，并非使用品。
 

青铜器铭文的字体主要为篆书，多铸刻在青铜器的内底、盖内。铭文始见于商代早期，商代中晚期逐步增多，但文字均较短，最短者仅一二字，最长者也不超过50字。内容大多较为简单，主要为所有者的族名、祭祀对象、作器者名、用途等。但已经具备结构、章法和用笔这三个构成书法艺术的要素。
 

妇好墓中出土的青铜礼器中有将近二百件有铭文，其中妇好和单一的“好”字一百件左右，根据同时期的甲骨卜辞推断，此墓的主人就是商王武丁的配偶“妇好”，其人虽为女性，但却是带兵征伐之将帅，不是考古证据即在眼前，实在觉得不可思议。

 几十年前安阳科考队发掘殷墟最初的目的为了获得甲骨，随着科考的深入，考古大家以为不应该局限于小屯一地，同时还应该探寻更多其他类型的殷商文化遗址，最终以梁思永为首的考察队在西北冈一带发现了若干大型墓葬，这些墓葬体量巨大，往往有四条墓道，虽经过反复盗掘，可墓葬区的遗物还是超乎想象的丰富，由此有理由断定，这些大墓是为商王的陵墓，这一发现当年极为轰动，不用说国内知名的学者纷至沓来，连法国汉学家伯希和也专程至此一探究竟，安阳的考古发掘，是由中国学者和中国学术机关第一次自发自主的考古工作，而第一次即有这样的成就，是之前谁都没有料到的，在苦难艰辛的时代，这实在是一种激发国民自尊自信的强心剂，难怪几十年后，已入耄耋之年亲历者回忆起来，激动之情还是那样溢于言表。
