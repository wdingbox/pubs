Origin of TBI

 

1. Background

Regarding the origin of Chinese characters, currently there are two types of models that we can summarize:

  1. Creation Model
    Based on the legends or some historical records, many scholars proposed that the Chinese characters were created by ancient sages, like Yu, CiangJie[Ciangjie]. However, such kind of model lacks scientific evidences and could not be verified.
     
  2. Evolution Model
    This Evolution Model believes that Chinese characters were gradually developed from a low level of some painting/drawing/symbols to high-level intelligent writing system thru natural selection. Such model can well explain how some of the pictograms could assembly to the objects we observed, but it also has many other issues that we could not explain; its archeological evidences are also flimsy.

    We could apply the Creation Model into Egypt hieroglyphics or Maya iconic writing development systems study. Since we have found a large amount of painting/drawing that associated with the characters in their writing system, although it was still arguable in scholars.

 

2. Revelation Model

There are three significant archeological facts about TBI:

  1. A remarkably large number of TBI were discovered extensively on a small areas at Anyang. No significant related other bones inscriptions could be found around it or any other remote places. The character density at Anyang is extremely higher than anywhere else.
     
  2. The highly intelligent mature TBI suddenly appeared in history at the beginning of Shang Dynasty. No significant other related TBI could be found before or after this period. There is no significant language evolution evidence before and during the several hundreds of Shang Dynasty. The writing system formation speed is like explosion.
     
  3. The turtle bones were used as writing media only during the TBI times. Before and after that, we could hardly find turtle bones as writing medias in history. 

These three archeological features are significantly different from any other archeological discoveries in China. The single spot in location, the sharp pulse in time, the perfect in complicated writing system, the quantity of the characters, the object-oriented design methods, full coverage in human thoughts, its foundation roles in whole Chinese cultures, as well as its connections to the biblical accounts, and so on, all of these factors drove us to suggest the Revelation Model -- the Chinese writing system was created at one time at Yingxu by sages or prophecies through the heavenly revelation that were similar to those described in the biblical accounts.
