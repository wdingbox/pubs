A Perfect Mistake

 

We have found 600,000 words on turtle bones unearthed at Yinxu. With manually hand inscriptions, no matter how carefully being prepared, it was impossible not to make any mistakes. Following word, which represent the turtle with six feet, is obviously an example.

 

Sample of Mistake in TBI
  TBI Turtle
Mistake (error)
Correct

 

We believe that there exist more mistakes in tbi no matter how intellegent they were. The issue is that it is hard for us to tell which is error and which is correct. This could bring us difficulties during the word reconization.

However we must not say every mistake is a foolish one. If we know that a feet is a cross, then the turtle with 6 feet would produce 4 more micro cross in this single word. Ison't that a perfect mistake?
