TBI Collections Process

The process to get the variant characters from about 150,000 pieces of bones is called collection process. Through collection process we got 46,635 variant styles of characters in TBI.

The archeologists, scholars have made great efforts to record all scriptions found in unearthed bones mostly in their published papers and works. We call this process of find the scriptions from unearthed bones as collection process. So far, the total number of collected characters is estimated to reach about 610,000.

Our reseah is setup based upon such results achieved by poineers of sholars. A Jiaguwen sql database is created with unique id of each tbi with a colunum jink and jnm for grouping, factoring as well as other processes.
