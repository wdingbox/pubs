Chinese Writings Development Process In History

 

# Development Process Chinese
Name		 Times Descriptions
1 TBI invention 甲骨文 1600BC Initally created, writen on bones.
2 Bronzation 金文 1200BC -- 800BC Widely used on bronzes.
3 Hanization 漢化 800BC -- 225AD

The large amount of grammarly string of characters began to emerged on bamboos or clothes in history during the formulation of Chinese thoughts in Autum Spring Worrior times, and formed a so-called PreQing culture. By the end of Han Dynasty (202BC--225AD) the character and text format became unified and stable. Its highly compressed format text is easy to copy but uneasy for utterance. Such classical Chinese is called WenYanWen文言文. After foreign Mongolian conquered China in Yuan dynasty(1271-1368), to distinguish other languages or culture, people began to call Chinese character as Hanzi漢字, people or race as Hanren漢人, Hanzu漢族[Hanzi_HanRen_Origin].
4 Buddhaismization 佛化 225AD -- 1919AD During nearly two thousands of years, especially after 635AD, Buddhaism translated foreign phrase flurished in Chinese documents and literature. Chinese Buddism thoughts were gradually established, injected into chinese culture and became the domain of Chinese culture. It made the Chinese writting text more utterable but less compressed.
5 White-Plainization 白化 1919 Affected by western, the oral speaking or idoms began to be widely used in official documents and literatures. The compressed text format were totally liberated to become white plain text, that is completely speakable and used until today.
6 Simplification 簡化 1956

1935年8月21日，中華民國教育部发布第11400号部令，正式公布《第一批简体字表》，采用了钱玄同所编《简体字谱》中的324个，在当时又称“手头字”，这是政府第一次大规模推行简化汉字。但1936年2月被通令“暂缓推行”。

1956年1月28日，国务院全体会议第23次会议通过了《关于公布〈汉字简化方案〉的决议》。1964年5月審定通過《簡化字總表》1986年10月10日重新发表《简化字总表》（共收2235个简化字），此表在中國大陸使用至2013年。
 
After 1956, there are total about 2235 of Chinese characters are simplified and in use in mainland China. However, in Taiwan, Hongkong, Sigapore and other places, the Chinese characters remain unchanged. So today, in the world, there are two versions of Chinese characters in use in world today. The original purpose of simplication is to ease the usage and learning of Chinese, however, its results make Chinese more difficult to learn.
7 Latinization 拉丁化 1958

1958年2月11日，第一届全国人民代表大会第五次会议批准颁布《汉语拼音方案》The pinying (the Latin letters) were officially introduced into chinese writing system for pronounciation notations. For the first time in history, it was possible and ready for Chinese writing system to be replaced by Latin letters.
